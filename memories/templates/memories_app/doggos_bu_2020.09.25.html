Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

The Momenta app doesn't cost anything. It's free! If you want the super wide angle capture option, you'll need to buy a special holder, which will be available soon.

My pets run all over the place. How will I keep them in frame?

Soon we will have the option to capture a very wide angle of action. You'll need to buy a holder from us, and use a second phone. We stitch together the video from both phones to create the super wide angle video.

How do I edit the clips. Is it hard?

There is a clip editor built into the app. It's super easy to use - you don't need to be an expert to use it. You'll be able to edit your clips in seconds.

What are the advantages of using Momenta?

Typically when you want to capture a moment with video, you have to know that something is about to happen so you can get your phone ready. But so often you don't know something is about to happen that you may want to get video of. Momenta solves that problem.

Why not just use the normal camera app on my phone?

If you use the camera app on your phone and set it to record, you'll end up with a huge video file. Then you'll need to find a video editing tool to create clips. Momenta makes it so much simpler. No huge video files. No complicated video editing software.

How easy is it to share video clips?

It's super easy! There are two ways to do it. You can invite friends and family into your group. They will then automatically see all the clips you create. You can also share individual clips with friends or family, or onto social media with just a few clicks.
